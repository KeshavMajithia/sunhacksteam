What is this website?

150 million people are homeless worldwide. Half the world lacks access to essential health services. And we, the creators of this website, want to change that. We believe if fellow human beings help one another, we can overcome all challenges and make the lives of every single human being happy.

Features

attach_money

"No-skill" jobs

Jobless? Skilless? Homeless? You have come to the right place. We offer a bank of jobs that require low to no skills for you to start working and finally afford a home.

Emergency Supplies

Emergencies can be tough, we understand. That's why if you are in serious need of last-minute supplies for an emergency, this website is for you!

Volunteers Needed

If you are a non-profit organisation and you lack manpower to perform your cause, fret not as we will help you find some volunteers. In that way, we hope your cause will benefit humankind in one way or another!
